Eirlyn Kingdom

The Eastern Continent

Overview

A large territory encompassing almost all of the eastern side of Elune, The Eirlyn Kingdom has the most widespread development within the realm, with cities and settlements spanning the entire territory. With a temperate climate, temperatures throughout the territory are predictable and constant which allows for a longer growing season than anywhere else in the realm. The lush grasslands and lively forests of the territory grow on very fertile land, providing the perfect place for a wide variety of crops to grow.

The Eirlyn Kingdom also has many exports and industries, the main being agricultural goods and raw materials such as crystals, ore, and minerals. Much of the land is perfect for growing a variety of foods and fibers for crafting, and Crystalspring Forest is home to the largest mines in Elune, Lumlita Caverns. This cave system is the only place within Elune to find such a wide variety of ore and minerals along with massive crystals. Protected fiercely by the Crystal Keepers who inhabit it, the Crystalspring Forest guards its resources from thieves, however are happy to trade with those who ask.

Places of Interest

    • Capital City
  • Cloveshire
    • Trading Epicenter
  • Crystalspring Forest
    • Crystal and ore mines, including the largest in Elune, Lumlita Caverns
  • Elderin Highlands
    • Alchemic Enclave
  • Lunarfall Basin
    • Agricultural Hub
  • Twinspeak Bluffs
    • Second largest location for landlocked Aqua Searchers
  • Azure Valley
    • Natural hot springs, rumored to have healing powers.

Kingdom of Iredale

The Western Continent

Overview

As the second largest kingdom in Elune, The Kingdom of Iredale is a diverse landscape from rolling plains, dense forests, rivers and lakes, to the harsh frozen mountains of the very west. Home to Hyperboreans who have ruled the tundras and snow capped cliffs and caverns for decades, very few Kulpera from other Talents dare to venture into the colder west. However, in the warmer southeastern part of Iredale, you can find Aqua Searchers at the sparkling Jade Lake, and Alchemists at home in Leidon Grove and Moonglad Marsh. Most of the inhabitants in The Kingdom of Iredale are spread throughout the land, with lots of untamed wilderness in between. Traversing this territory isn't for the faint of heart, however what you will find in its secluded regions is certainly worth the trek.

  • Frostport

  • Leidon Grove

  • Jade Lake
    • Largest body of freshwater in Elune, home to many landlocked Aqua Searchers
  • Tumblewind Prairie
    • Roaming Espyre herds
  • Moonglade Marsh
    • Mysterious forest, home to rural Alchemists

Kingdom of Astraeus

The Floating Continent

Overview

High in the clouds on a mysterious floating island, The Kingdom of Astraeus is home to the Star Seekers of Elune. Their territory doesn’t end in the clouds though, as they also govern the small island of Moonhaven in the Southern Sea. This region is heavily guarded by a royal family to protect the healing waters and fragile bioluminescent wildlife that thrives in this secluded area.

The Kingdom of Astraeus is by far the most technologically advanced kingdom in the Realm, with highly skilled doctors, astrologists, scientists, and other members of the High Society rank. Winged Alchemists are occasionally found there as well, practicing advanced alchemy. The Citadel itself is a bustling city, filled with Kulpera that are hungry for knowledge, and want to live an “elevated” lifestyle.

Places of Interest

  • The Citadel
    • Capital City
  • Moonhaven
    • Small island with unique properties and rare wildlife

The Oceanic Union

The Oceans and Seas

Overview

The Oceanic Union is made up of the oceans of Elune. Aqua Searchers of every shape and size live in independently governed regions, usually surrounding different biomes or reefs found in the seas and oceans. Each region has its own local government and Officials. Oceanic Officials help run the day to day of their region, and can make and remove local laws as they see fit. However, they may be overruled by the Oceanic Chancellor, and still need to get larger laws and ordinances passed through them. Oceanic Officials are voted on only by those living within their region once every solar cycle.

The Oceanic Chancellor moves to Emerald Cove upon election, and lives there for the duration of their term. Once every five solar cycles an election is held to vote for a new Chancellor, however there is no maximum a Chancellor may serve. Any important matters can be brought to Emerald Cove to be discussed with the Chancellor. Representatives from other territories can meet with the Oceanic Chancellor here as well, as Emerald Cove is attached to the mainland of The Erilyn Kingdom.

Places of Interest

  • Emerald Cove
    • Capital City
  • The Depths
    • The deepest part of all the waters in Elune, found in the Eirlyght Sea
  • The Ice Drifts
    • Large sheets of permanent sea ice to the northeast of The Kingdom of Iredale
  • The Solar Lagoon
    • Vast coral reef in the shape of a sun, teeming with life.

Tribes of the Brimstone Isles

Southern Volcanic Island Chain

Overview

Making up the volcanic island chain in the Southern Sea, The Brimstone Isles are characterized by a harsh and scorched environment. Forged by the relentless volcanic activity, the harsh landscape is barren aside from the occasional rivers and pools of lava. However, Volcano Explorers thrive here. The open pools of lava make for good blacksmithing, and those that reside there take full advantage of this, crafting some of the finest metalwork in all of Elune.

With barren soil, not much grows on the surface of this island chain, however a subterranean network of caves inside the larger volcanoes provide a home for its inhabitants. Fish are abundant in the warm waters surrounding the active volcanoes, and provide the Volcano Explorers with food, which is supplemented by trade, where they exchange their prized blacksmithed items for other necessities.

Places of Interest

  • Mount Brimshaw
    • The largest active volcano on the island chain, and in all of Elune.
  • The Brimstone Strait
    • The body of water that runs between Eirlyn and the mountain chain that makes up The Brimstone Isles.

Dragon Tamer Clans

The Eastern Continent

Overview

Within the clans of the Dragon Tamers, elders are the most respected. The Guardian Elder is chosen by the ancestor spirits, who visit them at their most sacred locations. Elder Dragon Tamers are always Transcended, and every younger Dragon Tamer’s goal is to learn from them and their ancestors to eventually transcend themselves. An immense amount of respect is shown to those who are older within Dragon Tamer Clans, with the Guardian Elders earning the most of all. Their words are taken as law, and each member of the clan will defend their Guardian Elder with their lives.

Places of Interest

  • Drakon Jungle
    • Where the Dragon Tamer Clans of the east reside. Even through there are no living dragons here, there is an ancient dragon skull where the Guardian Elders can contact the spirits.
  • Cloveshire
    • Trading Epicenter
  • Komorebi Woods
    • Where the Dragon Tamer Clans of the west reside. There is no sacred location within the woods here, however many more young dragons live within this forest, making it an ideal location for Dragon Tamers to live to gain further experience toward transcension.
  • Sacred Valley
    • Where the Guardian Elders of Komorebi Woods can contact the spirits, and where all Dragon Tamers go to transcend.

Protectors of the Forest

Homes of Wandering Spirits

Overview

The Elder Grove and the Hollow Forest are the only remaining places where soulies can be found. Forest Protectors guard them and guide them to the afterlife, staying deep within these dark forests. Even though they are located within other territories, the Royal Families of each Kingdom recognize the importance of the spirit realm, and as long as the Forest Protectors in the area are not causing trouble, they are left to do as they please. Elunians of all other Talents are advised to stay out of these forests, and those who dare to enter do so at their own risk, and with no protection from their ruling Kingdom.

Places of Interest

  • Elder Grove
    • A Dense and mysterious forest inside The Eirlyn Kingdom, which is always shrouded in darkness, the soulies providing light for Forest Protectors alone. Those who dare enter must bring their own source of light, otherwise risk getting lost within the trees.
  • Hollow Forest
    • Hauntingly beautiful forest made up of ghostly white trees inside The Kingdom of Iredale. The Forest Protectors who live here protect not only the soulies, but the trees as well. Hollowheart trees were once coveted for making ships and other crafts due to their magical properties. Heart of Elune

The Center of the Realm

Overview

Located in the very center of the realm, the Heart of Elune is an island between the eastern and western continents. Created by the gods of the realm, this neutral territory is a central hub for trade, meetings between kingdoms, and holding numerous festivals and events to be enjoyed by all. Dotted with crystals in hues of pinks and reds, they shimmer and sparkle with a glow that mirrors their very creators. Not only are they beautiful, but they radiate the magic that enhances the spiritual and mystical atmosphere of the Heart of Elune.

The Heart of Elune is home to the Heart Crystal, which was the very first piece of the realm to be created by Luna and Sol, a testament to their love and dedication to the realm. The Heart Crystal is a source of great power for the realm, and those who need to feel closer to their gods will make the journey to it to connect with them.

Places of Interest

  • The Heart Crystal
    • A large, heart shaped crystal that floats mysteriously above the ground. The Heart Crystal holds the most magic of any other source in the entire realm, leftover from when the gods Luna and Sol created it.
  • Festival Grounds
    • On the southern shore of the island is a large open field where the annual festivals are held. Close to the forest and the beach alike, it offers a clear view of the Heart Crystal.

The Badlands

A Desert Wasteland

Overview

Located at the southern tip of the eastern continent, beneath the borders of the Eirlyn Kingdom, The Badlands sprawl across a vast, hot, desolate desert. This neutral territory serves as a prison for Elune’s criminals, managed and guarded by The Order.

Surrounded by an impenetrable forcefield maintained by the secretive Alchemists of The Order, The Badlands are isolated from the rest of the realm. The Order also functions as a high court, wielding authority over justice and punishment, and stripping the talent of those who have committed the worst of crimes, turning them into Exileds.

Places of Interest

  • The Emberwatch Bastion
    • The stronghold where The Order resides
  • The High Court
    • Located in the Emberwatch Bastion, this is where all Exiling ceremonies take place.
  • The Badlands
    • Crystal and ore mines, including the largest in Elune, Lumlita Caverns
  • Elderin Highlands
    • Serving as the “jail” of Elune, this is where all Exileds will remain until they are redeemed, or perish.
